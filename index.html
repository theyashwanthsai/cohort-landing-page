The honest part

AI can do parts of your job.
It can't do the parts that took you 10 years.

AI CAN DO THIS TODAY
  • Write drafts. Test cases, reports, docs.
  • Sort and route. Tickets, defects, requests.
  • Run rule-based checks. First thing companies automate.
AI CAN'T DO THIS
  • Know your domain. Your clients, your systems, your rules.
  • Tell right from wrong. Someone experienced has to check.
  • Make judgment calls. That took you 10 years.

The plan: AI does the left column. You check its work. People who can do both are the ones companies keep.

I haven't coded in years (or ever). Can I do this?

Yes, that's the design assumption. The builds use AI-assisted coding. Your domain judgment is what matters.

Why do I have to post on LinkedIn?

The public, dated record is half the outcome. We help you draft every post.

Will my company data be involved?

No. Everything uses anonymized or synthetic material. We show you how in Week 0.

What if the timings don't work?

Sessions are recorded and most work is async. But if you'll miss most live sessions, wait for the next cohort.

Why only 15 seats?

Everyone gets a personal audit and projects picked for their job. That doesn't scale.
